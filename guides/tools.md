# Tools for Today

Everything you have access to during the workshop. You don't need to use all of these - pick what works for your problem.

---

## Think & Plan

Use these to brainstorm, research, and figure out your approach before building.

| Tool | What it's for | Best for | Link |
|------|--------------|----------|------|
| **Claude** (web) | Your main AI - ask it anything, plan with it, write with it | Deep thinking, long documents, complex problems | [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) |
| **AI Studio** | Google's free AI playground - experiment with Gemini models directly | Trying prompts quickly, comparing model outputs, free usage | [aistudio.google.com](https://aistudio.google.com) |
| **ChatGPT** | OpenAI's AI - great alternative, strong at structured tasks | Tables, data formatting, step-by-step plans | [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) |
| **Gemini** | Google's AI - connected to the web | Searching current info, Google Workspace tasks | [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) |
| **Grok** | xAI's AI - real-time and unfiltered | Current events, trending topics, quick takes | [x.ai/grok](https://x.ai/grok) |

| Tool | What it's for | Best for | Link |

## Present & Share

Use these to turn what you built into something you can show the room.

| Tool | What it's for | Best for | Link |
|------|--------------|----------|------|
| **NotebookLM** | Upload any document or link - it generates a summary, podcast, or study guide automatically | Turning your pod's notes into a shareable presentation | [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com) |
| **Your Pod Repo** | Your GitHub repo is your deliverable - it shows everything you built and thought through | The final presentation itself | See links in main README |

### How to use NotebookLM for your presentation

1. Go to notebooklm.google.com and sign in with a Google account
2. Create a new notebook
3. Upload your `notes.md` file, paste your GitHub repo link, or paste any text from today
4. Click "Generate" - it will create an audio overview (a fake podcast) summarizing your work
5. Use that as your presentation opener, or explore the other outputs it generates

## Create (Bonus)

If your pod's problem involves content, music, or video - these tools can help you build something impressive fast.

| Tool | What it's for | Best for | Link |
|------|--------------|----------|------|
| **Suno** | Type a description, get a full song back in seconds | Creating music for demos, ads, or content | [suno.com](https://suno.com) |
| **Udio** | Same idea as Suno - another AI music generator | Alternative if Suno is slow, slightly different style | [udio.com](https://udio.com) |
| **Runway** | AI video generation and editing | Creating short video clips from text or images | [runwayml.com](https://runwayml.com) |

---

## Quick Decision Guide

Not sure which tool to reach for? Use this:

- "I need to think through my problem" - Claude web
- "I need to actually build something" - Claude Code
- "I want to try something quickly for free" - AI Studio
- "I need current information from the web" - Gemini or Grok
- "I want to turn our work into a presentation" - NotebookLM
- "I want to make music or a jingle" - Suno
- "I want to make a video" - Runway
